AppJam+

AppJam is a program where 5 University students from both The University of California, Irvine and California State University, Fullerton go to a middle school and teach students how to code. We divide the kids up into 5 teams, each with a mentor, and create a game that promotes STEM for kids younger than them. In this program we as mentors learn the soft skills to have while working in a classroom with middle school students. We also learn techniques for encouraging and maintaining teamwork between the students in my team. I have seen these techniques be used in other team activities I have had.

MECHa's Cup

This was a soccer tournament where the proceeds would be used for a scholarship given out at the end of the year. This event required a year of planning and fundraising. Reserving fields for the tournament required money so I helped mobilize MEChA to help me fundraise for the tournament. I would plan out fundraising events and ask for people to sign up to help during the MEChA meetings. I would create itineraries and send them out to the people that said they would volunteer. The day of the tournament we hosted 8 teams, sold food, drinks and we raised enough money to give out 3 scholarships.

Vice President of Nu Alpha Kappa

As Vice President, I try to be the right hand of the president. I understand that the president needs to show his face at meetings for things we want to work on so he does not have time to be taking care of every situation. For that reason, I try to take care of things that are essential to the fraternity but are not in need of presidential supervision. Some examples include attending weekly Greek Presidents Meeting, monthly roster updates, connecting with the active/new members and representing the president when he is not available.

Vice President of Risk Management

As Vice President of Risk Management I created events that helped educate people about different topics related to assault, self-care and hazing. I want the MGC community to live a low risk life. This means a life where you can do whatever you want but you also have the knowledge to analyze risky situations. As long as I educate people tone aware of these risky situations and give them an idea of what to do then that makes me happy. I also revamped the MGC bylaws and constitution. They were very outdated, lacked structure and lacked bylaws that were needed to improve MGC trust and organization.
